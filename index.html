How to Create a Yahoo Mail Account

Follow these simple steps to set up your new Yahoo Mail account:

1

Go to Yahoo Sign Up Page

Visit Yahoo Account Creation page or click the "Create Account" button above.

2

Enter Your Personal Information

Fill in your full name, desired email address, password, birth date, and mobile number (optional but recommended for account recovery).

3

Verify Your Phone Number

If you provided a mobile number, Yahoo will send a verification code via SMS to confirm it's yours.

4

Complete Account Setup

Agree to Yahoo's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then click "Continue" to finish creating your account.

Tip: Choose a professional email address that you can use long-term, preferably with your name (e.g., john.smith@yahoo.com).

How to Login to Yahoo Mail

1

Go to Yahoo Mail Login

Visit mail.yahoo.com or open the Yahoo Mail app on your mobile device.

2

Enter Your Yahoo Email

Type in your complete Yahoo email address (e.g., yourname@yahoo.com) and click "Next".

3

Enter Your Password

Input the password you created during account setup. Check "Stay signed in" if using a personal device.

4

Access Your Inbox

Click "Sign In" to access your Yahoo Mail inbox and start sending/receiving emails.

Password Requirements

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • At least 1 uppercase letter
  • At least 1 number
  • No common words

Login Security Tips

  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Don't use public computers
  • Check for "https://" in URL
  • Log out from shared devices

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Account Creation Problems

  • Email already exists: Try variations of your preferred username
  • Weak password: Add numbers and special characters
  • Verification code not received: Check spam folder or request new code
  • Page not loading: Clear browser cache or try different browser

Login Problems

  • Forgot password: Use Yahoo's password recovery tool
  • Account locked: Wait 12-24 hours after multiple failed attempts
  • Browser issues: Update browser or disable extensions
  • App not working: Update or reinstall the Yahoo Mail app
Security Alert: Yahoo will never ask for your password via email. Always verify you're on mail.yahoo.com before entering login credentials.
